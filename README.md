📊 Sales Performance Dashboard

An end-to-end analytical solution that transforms raw retail transactional data into actionable business intelligence. This project leverages automated data engineering to provide real-time visibility into sales trends, product profitability, and customer behavior.

🚀 Overview
Retail businesses need to quickly identify which product tiers and customer segments drive revenue. This dashboard utilizes a custom data model to categorize products into four tiers—Economy, Standard, Premium, and Luxury—and segments customers based on their purchasing profiles, allowing for data-driven inventory and marketing decisions.

🛠️ Tech Stack
Data Preparation: Python (Pandas, NumPy) for cleaning and feature engineering.

Visualization: Power BI (DAX measures, Power Query) for interactive reporting.

Analysis: Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) focused on seasonal trends and customer lifecycle value.

📈 Key Insights & Recommendations
1. Product Strategy 🏷️
Cross-Tier Bundling: Utilize high-volume "Economy" items as entry points to upsell "Premium" and "Luxury" segments, increasing the Average Order Value (AOV).

Margin Balancing: Shift inventory focus toward "Luxury" tiers during high-spend months to maximize profitability.

2. Customer Retention 🤝
VIP Loyalty Program: Segregate high-impact customers to receive exclusive early access to new product releases.

Conversion Loops: Implement automated lifecycle campaigns targeting "New" customers within their first 30 days to accelerate repeat-purchase behavior.

3. Operational Efficiency ⚙️
Seasonal Demand Alignment: Align procurement cycles with the identified monthly sales dips and spikes to minimize holding costs and prevent stock-outs.
